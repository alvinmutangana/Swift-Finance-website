Who We Are

Swift Finance Ltd is a leading financial services provider based in Nyamata-Bugesera, Rwanda. We specialize in offering a wide range of loan products designed to meet the diverse needs of individuals and businesses across the country.

With a commitment to speed, transparency, and customer empowerment, we've built a reputation as a trusted partner for Rwandans seeking reliable financial solutions. Whether you're looking to buy a car, start a business, handle an emergency, or invest in property, we're here to help you every step of the way.
